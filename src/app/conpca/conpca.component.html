Continous Phishing Campaign Assessment

A phishing campaign assessment measures your team’s propensity to click on email phishing lures. Phishing is commonly used as a means to breach an organization’s network. Your team is phished once every 90 days until you opt-out, and the results can be used to provide guidance for anti-phishing training and awareness. Con-PCA provides a method to setup continuous subscriptions to phish your organization. Request information about the service(s) you are interested in by emailing.
