Cyber Competency Health and maturity progesion model

Cyber-Champ helps organizations to pinpoint exactly where to start improving their cybersecurity program using a step-by-step process that identifies the current cybersecurity posture, areas for improvement, and training paths that lead an organization to higher, measurable levels of cyber competency and maturity.

Benefits of Cyber-Champ

  • Achieve desired security maturity level
  • Increase security operational readiness
  • Increase bussines resilience
  • Develop and manage training profiles
  • Reduce organizational cyber risk
  • Maximize insurability
  • Minimize supply-chain risk
  • Increase ability to obtain and maintain a cyber workforce
